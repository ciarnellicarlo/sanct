Sunshine Animal Sanctuary

Our Mission

Our mission is to rescue and rehome animals in need. This includes abandoned, neglected and unwanted animals as well as those who are bred to be killed for consumption.
Our vision is to provide a haven for all animals to live out their days in a safe, peaceful and loving environment.
Standing up for those without a voice, our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate and to spread awareness of farm animals in need.
A haven for ALL animals to live out their days in a safe, peaceful and loving environment.

The Animals

Click to read their stories

Bianca

Previously belonged to a family who had to leave Malta unexpectedly and wished to find her a home as soon as possible.
She suffered form mange, which currently is being treated for and we are planning to introduce her to Nero in the near future in hopes they bond together.

Personality traits: Cute, sweet, friendly, curious and loving
Favorite foods: Parsley and carrots

Trixie

We adopted Trixie back in February 2020. She is a pot-bellied pig.

She is friendly, energetic, funny and hard-headed!

Serge

Was first spotted running around alone in the streets of Mosta. He was deemed a nuisance to neighbors for eating the farmers' grapes and being a hazard to passersby. This resulted in him being shot numerous times. We managed to catch him along with Animal Welfare but the law states that farm animals who aren't chipped, must go straight to the slaughterhouse in Marsa. We managed to convince the authorities that we could get him chipped and neutered.
He has had an x-ray which shows his right hind leg is full of pellets which unfortunately cannot be removed. Therefore, we are adding spices such as turmeric and black pepper to his food to help with the inflammation.

Cluck

Rescued from a house in Gzira.
He was living in a spare bedroom with many other animals including Pitbulls. When Animal Welfare came to confiscate all the animals, Cluck was rescued by us. He is now happy to roam around freely around the sanctuary.

Personality Traits: Expressive, loud, affectionate, funny, cute and cuddly

Scruff

Scruff is 1 out of 11 babies we have rescued along with their mother, from a rabbit farm. He was the runt of the litter. His name Scruff came from him being easily distinguished from his siblings due to his fur always being scruffed up and looking dirty.
He was the smallest out of his siblings, with health problems, so we have do keep him separate from others for a while. He's blossomed into a beautiful, healthy bunny and is living happily in the sanctuary.
